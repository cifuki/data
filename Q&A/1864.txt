Q:While on a break point, how do I see the call stack to find the callee method/function?
    
===============================================================================================
115
A:At the bottom panel you should have "5: Debug". Click on it and select "Debugger -> Threads"

You may need to find the "Threads" icon on the far right, or even click the "Restore Layout" button on the left to restore this window.
