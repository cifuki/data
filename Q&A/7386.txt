Q:I have this strange line wrap on Android Studio and I want to disable because it freaks me out...


    
===============================================================================================
51
A:In Android Studio 0.4.0 following should help:

Configure > Settings > Editor > Uncheck 'Use soft wraps in editor'




On Mac (Android Studio 3.0):

Preferences -> Editor -> General -> Uncheck 'Use soft wraps in editor'
